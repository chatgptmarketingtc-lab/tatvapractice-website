The Zyvelor Advantage
How Zyvelor powers TatvaCare

Our AI is self-improving through a virtuous cycle: TatvaCare products generate real clinical data, Zyvelor trains on that data to build smarter agents, which make our products more powerful.

1

Products Generate Data

Every interaction across TatvaPractice, GoodFlip, and Evalus creates real clinical data from real Indian healthcare workflows.

2

Zyvelor Trains & Optimizes

Our AI team uses this data to build, train, and continuously improve our AI agents with cutting-edge ML and Gen AI techniques.

3

Products Get Smarter

New agents and improvements are deployed back to products, making every feature faster, more accurate, and more clinical.
