Our Story
Founded by Zydus Group. Built for healthcare innovation.
2020
Founded by Zydus Group, India's leading pharmaceutical innovator. Started with a simple vision: AI that understands Indian healthcare.
2021
Launched TatvaPractice EMR with voice prescription support. First 5,000 doctors onboarded within 6 months.
2022
Introduced Zyvelor AI — 20+ clinical AI agents trained on real Indian healthcare data. Expanded to hospitals and diagnostic centers.
2023
Launched GoodFlip (Health & Wellness App) and Evalus (Real-World Evidence platform). Crossed 50K+ registered doctors.
2024-2025
Scaled to 200+ employees. Achieved 4.6x YoY growth. Expanded partnerships across India's healthcare ecosystem.
